Facilities

Our Spaces

Welcome to our state-of-the-art boxing facility, where champions are made and fitness goals are surpassed. Whether you're lacing up your gloves for the first time or you're a seasoned pro looking to refine your technique, our gym offers an unparalleled environment to train, sweat, and succeed.

Professional Boxing Rings

Step into the heart of our gym, where our professional boxing rings stand as the centrepiece. These rings are designed to meet professional standards, providing the perfect stage for sparring sessions, technical drills, and full-scale bouts. The high-quality canvas, sturdy ropes, and ample space mimic the feel of a real competition, offering an authentic experience for fighters preparing for their next big match or those looking to experience the thrill of the ring.

Dedicated Bag Area

Our dedicated bag area is where power meets precision. Featuring an array of punching bags, including heavy bags, speed bags, double-end bags, and more, this zone is designed for fighters to perfect their punches, kicks, and combos. The variety in bag types ensures that you can work on every aspect of your striking game, from power punches to rapid-fire combinations, in a space designed to withstand the most intense workouts.

Comprehensive Weight and Cardio Equipment

Our facility doesn't stop at the ring. We understand that a boxer's strength and endurance are built off the mat, which is why we've equipped our gym with a wide range of weight and cardio equipment. From free weights and resistance machines to treadmills and ellipticals, our selection caters to all aspects of physical conditioning. Whether you're aiming to bulk up, increase your stamina, or enhance your agility, our top-of-the-line equipment is at your disposal.

Clean, Modern Changing Rooms

We believe that comfort and cleanliness are key to a great gym experience, which is why our changing rooms are designed to offer a clean, modern space for you to prepare for your workout and unwind afterwards. Spacious, well-lit, and equipped with personal lockers, showers, and grooming areas, our changing rooms provide a private and relaxing environment for our members.

At our boxing gym, we're more than just a training facility; we're a community of fighters, trainers, and fitness enthusiasts committed to excellence. Join us and take the first step toward becoming the best version of yourself, inside and outside the ring.
