Beginner's Bootcamp

Our Beginner's Bootcamp is the perfect starting point for those new to boxing. This foundational course covers all the basics, from stance and footwork to punches and defense. Over the course of several weeks, you'll build a solid foundation in the sport, improve your fitness levels, and gain confidence in your abilities. The Bootcamp includes:

Group sessions focused on boxing fundamentals

Introduction to boxing equipment and how to use it safely

Basic fitness & conditioning exercises to build strength & endurance

A supportive, welcoming environment ideal for beginners

Fighter’s Programme

The Fighter's Programme is designed for individuals who are ready to take their boxing skills to the competitive level. This comprehensive training regimen focuses on advanced techniques, strategy, and the mental and physical preparation necessary for competition. Members of the Fighter's Programme can expect:

Advanced technical and tactical boxing training

Sparring sessions to apply skills in a controlled environment

Personalised coaching to refine techniques and strategy

Nutritional and mental fitness guidance to prepare for competitions

Warrior’s Workout

Our Warrior's Workout is a high-intensity fitness class that combines the best of boxing with strength and conditioning. This package is perfect for those who want to get in peak physical condition while enjoying the dynamic and engaging aspects of boxing. The Warrior's Workout includes:

Group fitness classes that incorporate boxing drills for a full-body workout

Strength and conditioning exercises tailored to enhance boxing performance

High-energy, motivational environment to push your limits

Suitable for all fitness levels, with modifications available for different abilities

$59/ m

Personal Training

For the ultimate customised experience, our Personal Training sessions are tailored specifically to your individual goals, whether that's weight loss, improving boxing technique, or preparing for a fight. Personal Training at Warriors Boxing Club offers:

One-on-one sessions with a dedicated coach

Customized training plan based on your personal fitness goals and boxing level

Flexible scheduling to fit your busy lifestyle

Continuous progress tracking and adjustments to ensure you're on the right path
