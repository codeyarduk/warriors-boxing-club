Meet Our Coaches

At Warriors Boxing Club, our coaching team is a dynamic blend of passion, expertise, and diverse cultural backgrounds, each bringing their own unique flavor to the art of boxing. These remarkable coaches are not just trainers; they are mentors who inspire, challenge, and empower our members to reach their full potential. From technical finesse to mental fortitude, they guide you through every aspect of boxing with dedication and skill. Get to know the exceptional individuals ready to support your journey at Warriors Boxing Club.

Antonio

Antonio combines his boxing prowess with his strength and conditioning expertise to offer a comprehensive training approach. His sessions are designed to enhance overall athleticism, focusing on strength, agility, and endurance. Antonio's philosophy centers on the symbiosis of a strong body and mind, preparing you for any challenge, in or out of the ring.

Jasper

Jasper is known for his intense, high-energy training sessions that epitomize his "train hard, fight easy" philosophy. A former pro boxer, Jasper challenges you to push beyond your perceived limits, fostering an environment where grit and determination thrive.

Caleb

Caleb's expertise in sports psychology gives him a unique edge in coaching. He excels at helping members overcome psychological barriers, building a resilient mindset that transcends the boxing ring. His empathetic and supportive style is especially encouraging for newcomers and those seeking to build confidence through boxing.

Yao

Originally from Thailand, Yao brings the rich boxing tradition of his homeland to Warriors Boxing Club. His style is infused with the fluidity and grace characteristic of Thai boxing, emphasizing agility and rhythmic movement. Yao's energetic and vibrant coaching approach makes each session not just a workout, but a celebration of movement and strength.

Matias

Matias is a former national champion whose love for boxing is matched only by his commitment to teaching. He emphasizes a strategic approach, focusing on outsmarting opponents with clever tactics and superior ring intelligence. His training sessions are a mix of physical rigor and mental chess, pushing you to your limits while sharpening your strategic thinking.
